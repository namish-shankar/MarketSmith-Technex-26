Game Protocol

Interface

  • All registered players will be divided into groups of six.
  • One common playing table will be shared among players of the group.
  • All players on the table can view the current question along with the holdings and PnL of other players.

Game Framework

  • Six digits (1-9) are generated at the start of each round.
  • The digits remain unrevealed throughout the round.
  • The PnL of every player at the start of the round will be set to zero.

Question Phase

  • One question appears on the dashboard at the start of every turn.
  • The correct answer corresponds to the digit for that turn.
  • Players must keep answers to themselves.
  • Answers must be kept with the player only.

Assets & Ownership

  • The value of one asset equals the sum of all six digits.
  • Each player starts with 2 assets.
  • An additional asset is given after the third turn.
  • There is no limit on maximum holdings per player.

Trading Rules

  • Players may quote bid and/or ask prices after solving the question in the 30-second window.
  • The orders set by players must be of one asset.
  • A trade is executed when bid and ask of two players are met.

Trading Logic & Execution Rules

  • If the highest bid is less than the lowest ask, no trades will be executed.
  • If the highest bid is higher than the lowest ask, trades will be executed following rules similar to a stock exchange.
  • Trades will be executed at the ask price.
  • Orders are matched from best bid and best ask outward.
  • In case of a tie, the player booking the order first will be given priority.
  • No money is kept by the dealer (sum of all playersâ€™ PnL is always zero).

End of Round

  • True asset value is revealed.
  • Final wealth = cash + asset valuation.
  • Players are ranked by total profit.
